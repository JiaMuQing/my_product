Chromium / Edge · Manifest V3

Account Switch

Cookie profiles · Snapshots & one-click switching

A Chromium-based extension for Chrome, Edge, and compatible browsers. It saves Cookie snapshots per site, with custom names and groups. When you switch accounts, it clears cookies for the current URL scope and restores the saved set. Data stays on your device by default.

Key features

  • Manage multiple accounts (profiles) per site with clear naming and groups
  • On switch, clears in-scope cookies and applies the snapshot to prevent cross-account leakage
  • UI follows the browser language; English and Simplified Chinese supported

The free tier limits how many accounts you can save (5).

Pro: $2 one-time lifetime subscription — save unlimited accounts.

Permissions & privacy

  • cookies, storage, tabs: read/write cookies, local storage, and tab context
  • Host permissions are requested as needed (per-site or broader), only for save/switch flows
